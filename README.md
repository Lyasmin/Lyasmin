# Larissa Yasmin QA Jr. Portfolio
## About me 👋
I am a Mechanical Engineer with solid experience in Quality within the automotive industry. I started my career as a Process Analyst, where I developed a critical view of production flows, failure identification, and continuous improvement.

Later, I worked in Supplier Product Quality, being responsible for evaluating components used in motorcycle assembly. In this role, I analyzed not only the product itself but also the full context of issues, investigating whether the root cause was related to manufacturing processes, assembly, handling, or operator actions.

When a defect was identified as product-related, I conducted technical analysis and created non-conformance reports (trouble reports), which were sent to suppliers for corrective actions.

This experience strengthened my skills in critical thinking, root cause analysis, technical communication, and quality assurance.
